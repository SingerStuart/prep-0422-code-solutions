The Spiderman Trilogy

Spiderman

Tobey Maguire gets bitten by a spider, gains spidey powers and learns how to fight crime with these powers. He learns that with great power comes great responsibility

Spiderman 2

Tobey's back at it again this time against Doc Oc, a mad scientist, and young James Franco who is looking for the love of Mary Jane

Spiderman 3

The last and arguably worst the worst Tobey Maguire spider installment where he becomes emo, physically assualts his girlfriend, and fights Sandman, Venom, and himself.
